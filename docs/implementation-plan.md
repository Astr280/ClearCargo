# Implementation Plan

## Phase 1: Core MVP

- Tenant and user auth
- RBAC and session controls
- Shipment CRUD and milestone tracking
- Document generation for core freight docs
- AR invoice creation and payment recording
- Customer portal for tracking and document access
- Dashboard and global search

## Phase 2: Compliance and Finance

- HS lookup and duty engine
- AES, ISF, and CBP 7501 workflows
- Sanctions screening
- AP and vendor matching
- Multi-currency accounting
- Gross profit analytics

## Phase 3: Transport and WMS

- Carrier schedules and rate cards
- Consolidation and co-load support
- Detention and demurrage tracking
- Warehouse receiving, put-away, picking, and dispatch
- Driver and warehouse mobile apps

## Phase 4: Enterprise Scale

- Full white-label tenancy
- Usage-based billing
- ERP and EDI integrations
- Advanced analytics and custom reports
- SLA monitoring and enterprise controls
